★ LIVERBLUEPEDIA ★

Liverbluians - Liverblue District denizens.

Liverblue House - Building in the district for Liverblueians who are too lazy to go back to their home outside the district, especially if their home is faaaaar away from the district. Each Liverblueians gets their own room in the building and they can customize it however they like.

Superastra Space Agency - The space agency Hualing and Hetansh (and some other people) used to work in.

Emerald Aerospace Company - Aerospace company in the district owned by Beiming.

Batterymart - Battery store in the district owned by Lina.

Linewave - Social media site made by Silvestre for the Liverbluians.
